BUNDELKHAND'S BIGGEST TECHFEST

Innovanza is an annual technical and innovation festival organized by Bundelkhand Institute of Engineering & Technology (BIET), Jhansi. This festival serves as a vibrant platform for students to showcase their technical skills, innovative ideas, and creativity.
The primary aim of Innovanza is to foster a culture of innovation, entrepreneurship, and technical excellence among students. The festival features a diverse range of events including technical competitions, workshops, guest lectures by industry experts, project exhibitions, and panel discussions on emerging trends in technology.
